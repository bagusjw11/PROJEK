Our Services

Services

BLOOD CHECK

A blood check is a series of examinations using a blood sample obtained through a vein in a certain part of the body or from a wrong stab.

VACCINE

Vaccines are inactivated or attenuated antigens (microorganisms) which, when given to healthy people, produce specific antibodies.

SWAB TEST

Swab antigens, also known as rapid antigens, work by detecting certain proteins from viruses that evoke an immune response.

CONSULTATION

General consultation, also known as health consultation, is a meeting between a doctor and his patient. The aim of this consultation is to carry out preventive measures to stop the development of various diseases for patients who have risk factors.

Outpatient / Polyclinic

Outpatient care is a medical service for patients for the purpose of observation, diagnosis, treatment, rehabilitation and other health services without requiring the patient to be hospitalized.

Inpatient

Hospitalization is a form of treatment or rehabilitation process by professional health care workers for sick patients, by being hospitalized in a room that is adjusted to the type of disease they are experiencing.
